About Us

Brighter Minds Academy LLC is not your average childcare center!!! It is an innovative, creative, educational facility that seeks to empower its learners by providing a safe, family-friendly atmosphere that values diversity, and incorporates a barrier free environment for learning. BMA strives to build fundamental principles and the life skills necessary to inspire, challenge the growth of children of all ages.

Mission Statement

To provide families with a high-quality child-care option where parents and teachers work together in raising the next generation.
